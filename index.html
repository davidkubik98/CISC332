What is QICC?

QICC is a non profit conference event organized annually for students, by students from across Canada. Our mission is to educate and expose students to a diverse range of areas in software engineering and computer science. We empower them to discover knowledgeable speakers, connect with sponsoring companies, and make lifelong friends, all in a safe and comfortable space. We take great pride in hosting unique and knowledgeable speakers that come from many backgrounds in the software industry. Notable past speakers include Alexis Ohanian, Jeff Hodges, Richard Stallman, Julia Evans, and Liz Abinante.
